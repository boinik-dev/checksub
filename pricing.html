Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay?

Checksub accepts all major credit cards, including VISA, MasterCard, AMEX, Discover and more. We offer other custom billing solutions for high volume.

Can I pay by wire transfer?

Yes, it's possible. In the case of a project it will start once your payment is received. If you wish to purchase minutes your account will be credited once payment is received.

How long are the purchased minutes valid for?

The minutes purchased are valid for 1 year. If you have not had the time to use them during this period they will unfortunately be deducted from your account. It is not possible to get a refund.

What’s the difference between Premium and Essential minutes?

The main difference is the available export options. With Premium minute you can export the video without watermark and you can download the source file (SRT,VTT,...). With Essential minutes you can export the video but with a discrete watermark.
